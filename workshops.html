Classes

Over the weekend, two teaching couples will guide you through a series of workshops covering everything from fundamentals to styling and musicality. On Saturday, we'll kick things off with a friendly all-level audition to sort everyone into two groups — Plätzchen and Punsch — so you end up exactly where you'll have the most fun and get the most out of each class.

Both groups get the same amount of floor time with each teaching couple, and the levels are designed to make sure everyone feels comfortable and challenged at the same time. No stress, no pressure — just good dancing.

Seminar — From Dance Goals to Action!

Ever feel like you're putting tons of energy into your dancing without a clear sense of where it's all heading? This seminar-style workshop with dance coach Vanessa Bachmann helps you find your direction.

Drawing on her background in health and wellness coaching, Vanessa guides you through a practical, three-part process: first, an honest look at why you dance, the kind of dancer you want to be, and what truly motivates you. Then, a playful exercise to surface your real goals and the vision for your future dancing self. Finally, you'll turn those insights into clear focus areas, prioritize them, and walk away with one concrete step you can take the very next day.

Whether you're chasing connection, confidence, musicality, technique, or your own personal style — you'll leave with clarity about your path and a renewed sense of joy in your practice.

You'll also receive a workbook to keep working with on your own.
