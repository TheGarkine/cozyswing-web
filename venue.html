Venue

Hep Cat Club, Augsburg

The Hep Cat Club has become our second living room over the last few years — and we can't imagine a better place for CozySwing. It's right at Augsburg Hauptbahnhof, tucked away on the 1st floor next to Platform 1. One of our favourite things about the space is the big glass front facing the train tracks — it fills the room with light during the day and gives it a really special atmosphere at night.

There's plenty of room to dance, a cozy bar area to hang out between classes, and everything you need for a good weekend. If you've been here before, you know the vibe. If you haven't — you're in for a treat.

Address

Hep Cat Club

How to find us

From Augsburg Hauptbahnhof, walk between Subway and Kokono (Asian restaurant), head right toward Platform 1, and take the last or second-to-last door on the right. Look for the Hep Cat Club logo on the door — then up the stairs to the 1st floor.
