Your time to relax right before Christmas!

CozySwing is a West Coast Swing weekend in the heart of Augsburg. From December 18th to 20th, join us for three days of workshops, social dancing, and good company — the perfect way to wind down before the holidays.

Two teaching couples will guide you through the weekend. Workshops are split into two groups for the perfect experience — we'll sort everyone in a friendly audition on Saturday so you end up exactly where you'll have the most fun.

Expect 9+ hours of workshops, 20+ hours of social dancing, a charity Jack & Jill competition, pro shows, and plenty of surprises — all packed into one cozy weekend.

Beyond the dancing, CozySwing is about the atmosphere — cozy vibes, warm drinks, and a welcoming community. Whether you're traveling from afar or joining from around the corner, we can't wait to share this weekend with you.

Registration opens soon — stay tuned!

A Bit of History

CozySwing has been around since 2015 — it was the first West Coast Swing event for many of us here in Augsburg, and a highlight of the December calendar for years. After COVID put it on pause, we're finally bringing it back. The format has evolved, but the spirit is the same: good people, good music, and a cozy weekend of dancing right before Christmas.

Charity Jack & Jill

On Saturday night, we're hosting a Jack & Jill competition — and it's all for a good cause. Entry fees go straight to charity. It's a true Jack & Jill: all levels are welcome, partners are drawn at random, and it's all about having fun on the floor. Whether you're competing or cheering from the sidelines, it's going to be a great time.

Afraid You'll Have to Organize Last-Minute Christmas Presents?

No panic — Saturday morning is free for last-minute shopping in Augsburg's city centre. We've put together a map with our favourite local spots and there'll be a gift wrapping station at the venue. Check out our recommendations.
